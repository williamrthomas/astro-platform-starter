Rhythm Defense

How to Play

Rhythm Defense is a unique hybrid of tower defense and rhythm game mechanics.

1. Place towers strategically on the grid to defend against waves of enemies

2. Activate towers by pressing their number keys (1-4) in sync with the beat

3. Perfect timing gives 2x damage, good timing gives 1.5x damage

4. Build combos for score multipliers and earn resources by defeating enemies

5. Survive all 10 waves to win!
